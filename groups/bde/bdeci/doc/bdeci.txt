/Hierarchical Synopsis
/---------------------
 The 'bdeci' package currently has 2 components having 2 levels of physical
 dependency.  The list below shows the hierarchical ordering of the components.
..
  2. bdeci_hashtable

  1. bdeci_hashtableimputil
..

/Component Synopsis
/------------------
: 'bdeci_hashtable':
:      Provide a container of unique 'T' values.
:
: 'bdeci_hashtableimputil':
:      Provide implementation support for precomputed hashtable sizes.
