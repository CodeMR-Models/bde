@PURPOSE: Provide concrete TCP/IPv4 channels derived from 'btesc' protocols.

@MNEMONIC: Basic Transport Environment SOcket-based Streams (btesos)

@AUTHOR: Tom Marshall (tmarshal)

@DESCRIPTION: The 'btesos' package provides concrete imlpementations of 'btesc'
 channels using TCP/IPv4 sockets.  Timed and untimed, and blocking and
 non-blocking versions of generic channels, acceptors, and connectors are
 provided.
